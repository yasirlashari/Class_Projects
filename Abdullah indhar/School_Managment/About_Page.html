About Al-Ilm School

Al-Ilm School aims to provide quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our mission is to foster a love for learning and equip students with the skills they need to succeed in life.

Founded in 2000, we have grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the region, offering classes from Nursery to Class X.

Our dedicated team of teachers and staff work tirelessly to create a nurturing environment where every student can thrive.
